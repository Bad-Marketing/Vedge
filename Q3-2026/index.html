Q3 2026 Strategy

VEDGE NUTRITION

Expanding Revenue Contribution Across the Catalog

Prepared by BAD Marketing  ·  Amazon Department

Strategic Objective
Expand Revenue Contribution Across the Catalog

Collagen continues to be the brand's strongest driver of customer acquisition, repeat purchases, and Subscribe & Save growth. The opportunity for Q3 is to turn more existing customers into multi-product customers and increase the contribution of secondary products across the catalog.

Rather than focusing on conversion rate optimization or retention improvements — which are already healthy — the priority is to expand growth across Creatine and Protein by improving product discovery, increasing multi-product adoption, and introducing new customer acquisition channels.

By increasing adoption of Protein and Creatine products, Vedge can create additional growth drivers while continuing to build on the success of Collagen.

Growth Area 1: Build Creatine into a Meaningful Revenue Driver
Opportunity

Vedge has established strong visibility within the Vegan Collagen category, but remains underrepresented within the significantly larger Creatine category.

Both Creatine+ and Pure Creatine demonstrate healthy conversion rates, however their contribution to overall account revenue remains relatively small compared to the size of the category opportunity.

The goal for Q3 is to turn Creatine into the second major growth driver within the account.

Recommendations
  • Clearly define the role of Pure Creatine versus Creatine+ to minimize shopper confusion and improve product discovery.
  • Position Pure Creatine as the daily creatine solution while positioning Creatine+ around enhanced performance, stamina, and focus support.
  • Increase visibility of Creatine products throughout the catalog, particularly across Collagen and Protein listings.
  • Integrate and increase Creatine visibility within Brand Story and Storefront.
  • Expand non-branded Creatine keyword coverage through SEO and advertising.
  • Leverage Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Display to introduce Creatine products to existing Collagen and Protein customers.
Product Positioning
1
Pure Creatine — Daily Creatine Solution
  • Entry-level, daily creatine habit
  • Positioned for broad creatine category capture
2

  • Elevated performance, stamina and focus support
  • Positioned for fitness-forward, performance-focused customers
Growth Area 2: Increase Customer Value Through Multi-Product Adoption
Opportunity

Vedge has successfully built a loyal customer base, particularly around Collagen. However, customer behavior remains heavily concentrated around individual products rather than broader catalog adoption.

The opportunity is to increase customer lifetime value by introducing existing customers to complementary products that naturally fit within their wellness journey.

Rather than acquiring entirely new customers, Q3 should focus on helping existing customers expand their relationship with the brand.

Reevaluate Product Roles Across the Catalog

Q3 presents an opportunity to revisit product roles and ensure they accurately reflect how customers currently discover, purchase, and repurchase products.

Supporting Actions
  • Build routine-based messaging across listings, Brand Story, A+, and Storefront.
  • Create a consistent "Complete Your Routine" section across Collagen, Protein, and Creatine products.
  • Develop a dedicated Collagen → Protein → Creatine journey within the Store.
  • Use Sponsored Display and Sponsored Brands audiences to introduce existing customers to the next stage of the journey.
  • Evaluate Virtual Bundle opportunities that support product discovery and multi-product adoption.
Growth Area 3: Leverage Creator Connections as an Additional Acquisition Channel
Opportunity

Creator Connections presents an opportunity to drive incremental revenue, acquire new-to-brand customers, and increase visibility of secondary products across the catalog. It can also serve as a key driver of the Customer Journey Framework by introducing existing customers to Protein and Creatine products through routine-based content.

Recommendations
  • Launch Creator Connections campaigns focused on NTB acquisition and revenue generation.
  • Develop creator briefs aligned with the Customer Journey Framework:
    • Feel Your Best (Collagen)
    • Fuel Your Body (Protein)
    • Build Strength (Creatine)
  • Test multi-product campaigns showcasing how products work together.
  • Measure NTB sales, revenue, and ROAS.
  • Establish Creator Connections as a repeatable acquisition channel.
Advertising
Opportunity

Performance data shows strong efficiency from Exact, Phrase, and Broad match types on Sponsored Products, with ACOS as low as 6–12% in early testing. This signals a clear opportunity to scale these match types further to drive more efficient growth.

Creatine+ and Pure Creatine are also performing efficiently, positioning them well for increased investment as we build Creatine into a larger growth driver.

Recommendations
  • Expand Sponsored Products Exact, Phrase, and Broad campaigns to capture more category ranking and drive efficient, scalable growth on core keywords.
  • Layer in additional budget for Creatine campaigns to build on its strong early efficiency and accelerate its growth as outlined in Growth Area 1.
  • Use Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Display to introduce Collagen and Protein customers to Creatine, supporting the Customer Journey Framework (Collagen → Protein → Creatine).
  • Continue refining campaign structure around product roles, so each match type and ad format plays a clear, intentional part in the broader strategy — discovery, ranking, and retention.
